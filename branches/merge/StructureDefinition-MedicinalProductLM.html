Logical Model: Medicinal product (model)

Official URL: http://profiles.ihe.net/PHARM/MPD/StructureDefinition/MedicinalProductLM Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2025-03-23 Computable Name: MedicinalProductLM

Copyright/Legal: IHE http://www.ihe.net/Governance/#Intellectual_Property

Logical model for prescribed/dispensed medication. The model is shared by statements, requests, dispensations, and treatment lines. Each of those may have different restrictions in FHIR profile.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Logical Model Profile: Medication order - multiline (model) and Medication dispensation (model)
